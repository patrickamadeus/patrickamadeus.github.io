I am a Research Engineer in the NLP group at Singapore Management University, working in Prof. [Chong-Wah Ngo's](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=HM39HrUAAAAJ&hl=en) lab. I am also an incoming Ph.D. student in NLP at _(undisclosed)_. Previously, I completed my Bachelor in Computer Science at Institut Teknologi Bandung under the supervision of [Prof. Ayu Purwarianti](https://scholar.google.co.id/citations?user=8jUro_cAAAAJ&hl=id) working on explainable multimodal synthetic data generation. I have published and reviewed works in multiple conferences such as EMNLP, NAACL, and COLING.

My current research interests lie primarily in the areas of Multimodality, Large Language Models (LLMs), and Multilingual & Multicultural NLP. I am the member of [SEACrowd](https://seacrowd.github.io/) and [Grassroots Science](https://grassroots.science/) initiatives. Come join us! We are still early!

(Soon) I will be VERY active on [X](https://x.com/patrickamadeus_) during my doctoral journey. Please follow me there to reach out, connect, or exchange ideas!
